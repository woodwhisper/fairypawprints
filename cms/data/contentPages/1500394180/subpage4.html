Fairy sized Reviews

Hunger Games.

I had just finished the novel a couple of days ago and found it to be very good. The movie is not quite as good, though still good enough in its own right that I don't really have anything bad to say. It gives us an insight into things that the main character, Katniss Everdeen, doesn't know about (as the novel is in the 1st Person, and therefore the PoV is limited), but the downside of that is we don't get as much of an insight to Katniss and her thoughts. One moment in the movie literally brought me to tears. Kudos to the screenwriter and the director. I give this film a 4 out of 5.

GI Joe: Retaliation.

For a film based on a toy series, this was definitely one of the better ones. I really enjoyed the story, though some of the stuff was a little silly (like the sandbuggy/tank cross that kicked Cobra's tanks all over the place). I give it 3 out of 5.

Hunger Games (Book 1, yes the novel)

I finished reading The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins early this morning... It was pretty darned good. Though generally, I find that kind of dystopia to be a little on the unbelievable side, the tight writing and great characterisations (in 1st person PoV no less!) made this a book that is a must read.
