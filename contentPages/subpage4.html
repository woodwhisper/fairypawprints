Hunger Games (Book 1, yes the novel)

I finished reading The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins early this morning... It was pretty darned good. Though generally, I find that kind of dystopia to be a little on the unbelievable side, the tight writing and great characterisations (in 1st person PoV no less!) made this a book that is a must read.
