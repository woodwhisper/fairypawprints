Ten years ago, super powered individuals appeared in the world.
Sergeant Kerry Jennings is just your average career military woman, until she is abducted and transformed by the super criminal Iron Sting, and set the task of returning his most successful experiment to him. Unfortunately for him, his experiment is not willing to return...
